Services

  • Registration

    Professors may sign in using a valid UWindsor username/password to create and maintain their personal website. University of Windsor students can also login to upate and maintain their GA/TA information.

  • Courses

    Professors have the ability to add and change any courses that they are currently teaching or have taught in the past. Users can read this information without needing to login to learn more about different professors. Within different courses, professors can upload files and assignments. Addtiotnally, the professor can create annoucements specific for courses to update the students.

  • Student Management

    Professors can manage the students that are TA/GA's for differnt courses they are teaching. The different students can be added, deleted or contacted by the professors.

  • Publications

    Professors can upload or link any publications or research they are working on which can be viewed by those that look through ProfBook.
