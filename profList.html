Roohollah Etemadi

Professor Description:

Roohollah Etemadi is a PhD candidate at the School of Computer Science at the University of Windsor, and working under the supervision of Professor Jianguo Lu and Professor Yung H. Tsin. His research interests are mainly in big data analytics, estimation, scalable algorithms, machine learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, and graph/text mining. His previous research focus was in the areas of data/web mining, computational intelligence, and evolutionary computation.

Department:
